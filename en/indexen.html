Design Studio

Design Studio is a creative online studio that offers a wide range of design services to provide clear and professional solutions — from idea to final result. My work is based on understanding client needs, precise communication and attention to every detail.

Visual & promotional materials

I create visual and promotional materials for both personal and business needs — logos, business cards, brochures, flyers and designs for branded items such as lighters, pens, T‑shirts, mugs and more. Every design is adapted to its purpose, target audience and brand identity.

Cards, invitations & thank‑you notes

I design cards, invitations and thank‑you notes for business events, personal celebrations and special moments — with a focus on style, tone and visual harmony, so that every piece looks thoughtful, neat and professional.

Copywriting & presentations

Design Studio also offers writing services — promotional texts, speeches, themed articles and other formats. I create clear, well‑structured presentations adapted to professional settings, whether for meetings, clients or public speaking.

Frequently asked questions

How long does a project take?

Business cards: up to 2 working days. Logo: 5–7 working days. A website: 2–4 weeks depending on scope. Exact deadlines are agreed at the start.

How does payment work?

Usually 50% deposit at the beginning and the rest upon completion. For larger projects, instalments are possible.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Collaboration is possible in Serbian or English via email, Viber or video calls.

What do I need to get started?

Basic idea, references (if you have them), texts and key information about your brand. We can shape the rest together.

Do you take urgent projects?

Depending on current workload, faster delivery may be possible with an extra fee. We clarify this at the beginning.

What’s included in a website price?

Typically design, development, responsive layout, basic SEO and post‑launch support. Domain and hosting are paid separately.

How many revisions are included?

Up to two revision rounds are standard for most projects. Additional revisions can be agreed separately.

How do you deliver final files?

Via download link or email. Printed materials can be prepared for print, while finished cards can be sent by post or picked up in person, depending on what we agree.
