About Me

I believe that true personal development and professional growth come from diverse experiences, curiosity, and the courage to explore the unknown. Openness to new challenges has shaped my life and career approach: during high school, I participated in numerous projects that developed creative, intellectual, and organizational skills. I competed in national Serbian language competitions, placing among the top three participants three times for essays on historical figures and creative topics, edited the school electronic magazine, and managed school news, while also developing skills in acting and culinary arts. A notable project was within Management, where I independently created a brochure – a tourist guide for Despotovac municipality, prepared texts, photos, and visual materials, which won first place in a local competition and showed me how dedication and precision bring great results. The combination of professional experience, versatile skills, leadership abilities, communication proficiency, and openness to new experiences allows me to approach every project with passion, innovation, and dedication, leaving a positive and lasting impact on the communities I work with.

Although initially shy and uneasy with public speaking, experience and interaction with people allow me to gradually open up, expressing ideas with confidence, clarity, and authenticity.

I have been actively working on design for two months, and during this period, I founded my own studio and began independent work. Although it was a short start, interest and demand for my services quickly appeared, giving me additional confidence and motivation to continue building my professional path. I approach every project with great dedication, a desire to learn, and continuous skill improvement. I believe in high-quality work, clear communication, and responsibility – values upon which I build my studio and long-term collaborations.

Work Experience

My professional journey reflects the same passion and curiosity. As a Tourist Guide in Resava Cave, I led groups of over 2,000 visitors during holiday periods, created promotional materials, wrote texts about the cave, designed brochures, managed social media, and handled ticket sales. My desire to explore new fields led me to experiment with flavors – as a bartender, I developed innovative cocktails, and later discovered latte art as a barista, improving precision, aesthetics, and customer interaction. In parallel, through my Design Studio, I honed project management skills, developed creative concepts, and implemented original ideas, while also teaching myself to code websites.

My Goal

My ultimate goal reflects the belief that knowledge and resources should be accessible to everyone: I want to create a village where all homeless people can live with dignity and receive free meals every week, as well as act through educational initiatives – providing free books to children of single mothers and funding their education, offering opportunities for growth and development to those who need help most.

Education

I completed high school at Technical School in Despotovac, Tourism-Hospitality Technician track, and in the Oxford entrance exam (Philology English program) I was among the top three candidates with 90 points. Although I did not continue formal university education, I chose to develop through practical experience and independent creation, with a clear vision to create projects that reflect values, creativity, and professional autonomy.

Trivia - All About Me

Versatility and an investigative spirit define my identity. Photography, drawing, writing, culinary arts, manicure, tattooing, and self-piercing are part of my expression and the activities I explored, developing precision, patience, and innovative thinking. I pay special attention to studying different cultures and traditions, reading literature, and watching documentaries that deepen understanding of the world, customs, and values, enhancing my adaptability and success in multicultural environments. Besides Serbian and English, I started learning basic Korean but had to pause due to guiding work and time constraints; my goal was to expand communication horizons.

MONASTERIES

I love visiting monasteries, local churches, and monastery viewpoints – they provide peace and inspiration. I often visit them during holidays or Sundays, enjoying the quiet.

HIKING

I love hiking and visiting viewpoints, as being in nature brings me peace and new energy. Every ascent is an opportunity to step away from daily life and see things from a different perspective.

LANDMARKS

I enjoy visiting local cultural and natural landmarks I have not seen before. My goal is to explore all of Serbia first, many of its beauties I have already had the chance to discover.

ANIMALS

I particularly love animals – both domestic and exotic. Over time, I had various pets including a Vietnamese pot-bellied pig, a crab, a guinea pig, and several other exotic species.

CUISINE

I enjoy exploring local flavors and trying traditional specialties from places I visit. Every meal is a part of the cultural experience and an opportunity to learn about local customs and traditions. My favorite dish comes from Bajina Bašta – roasted kaymak, which I always particularly enjoy.

NATURE

I love nature and believe it is important to protect it, recycle, and not harm it. My favorite tree is the weeping willow, which reminds me of my school park and the abstract graffiti art around it, inspiring the idea for Design Studio.

FASHION

I love fashion, especially autumn collections, when I can combine coats, layered outfits, and styled hats. Autumn gives the perfect opportunity to experiment with colors, materials, and details, creating outfits that are both elegant and comfortable.

LANDSCAPES

I particularly enjoy landscapes – observing, photographing, and drawing them. I often find inspiration in nature, through photos and writing posts dedicated to the natural environment.

TARA

My favorite mountain is Tara, where I enjoy every winter moment. I love seeing deer and the friendly locals. Tara is busy but has kept its authentic character, which makes me return happily every winter.

RESAVA CAVE

I spent a year as a tourist guide in Resava Cave, helping me overcome public speaking fear. After that, I worked in marketing, creating brochures for the cave, discovering how much I enjoy design and creating visual materials.

VELIKI KRŠ

I am not a big fan of summer tourism, but I enjoy mountain trips in summer, especially Veliki Krš and the surroundings. I also love spending summer at Gruža Lake, where locals are always very friendly.

ART

I love canvas artwork, as it inspires and expresses what words often cannot. Each canvas tells its own story, colors, and emotions, and I enjoy observing every detail shaping a unique artistic vision.

TRAVEL

Travel is my greatest inspiration – I love discovering new cultures, cities, and ways of life. Every new place brings a different perspective and ideas I later bring into my work.

MOSTAR

My favorite nearby travel spot is Bosnia, especially Mostar for its special atmosphere and colorful streets. I enjoy the street markets and local specialties and desserts with traditional flavors. Every visit is a chance to explore the culture and absorb the charm of this beautiful city.

Design Studio

Design Studio is a creative online studio providing a wide range of design services to deliver clear and professional solutions — from concept to final execution. Our work is based on understanding client needs, precise communication, and attention to every detail.

Visual and Promotional Materials

We create visual and promotional materials for personal and business needs. Our services include logos, business cards, brochures, flyers, and designs for branded items like lighters, pens, t-shirts, mugs, and more. Cards, Invitations, and Thank You Notes

We also design greeting cards, invitations, and thank-you notes for various occasions — business events, personal celebrations, and special moments — focusing on style, tone, and visual harmony. Our goal is for each material to look thoughtful, neat, and professional.
